# 🔧 Algorithms

### The Concept (Simple Explanation)
An algorithm is simply a **recipe** for solving a specific problem. It's a step-by-step set of instructions. Your data structures are the ingredients, and the algorithms are the steps you follow to cook up a solution.

### Your Roadmap
- **Sorting & Searching**: The most fundamental recipes that every programmer must know.
- **Graph Algorithms**: Recipes for solving problems related to networks (like finding the shortest route on a map).
- **Dynamic Programming**: A powerful technique for solving complex problems by breaking them into smaller, reusable sub-problems. It's tough, but worth it!
- **Backtracking**: A methodical trial-and-error approach to explore all possible solutions to a problem.

**Professor's Tip:** Don't just memorize algorithms. *Understand* them. Trace them on paper with a small example. Ask "why" at every step. This deep understanding is how you'll be able to adapt them to new problems.
